What it does

Purpose-built for Victorian Curriculum data

Any reporting system, one click

Imports the standard Compass semester export (CSV) directly, or a simple fill-in template for schools on other systems — no re-typing, no spreadsheet surgery. Import one semester or a whole folder of years in one go. Re-importing safely replaces a semester and shows exactly what changed, a data-health check flags whole-class entry errors and improbable jumps automatically — and attendance, PAT and NAPLAN files get a pre-import match preview showing exactly who matched before anything lands.

Growth you can see

Every result is banded against the semester-aware "at level" expectation across seven colour-coded bands. Compare any two points in time — the six-months-per-semester expectation scales automatically. "Made growth" percentages at a glance. Prep to Year 10, every Victorian Curriculum learning area.

Every student's story

Click any name for their full history: every subject and semester, colour-coded, with summary statistics — attendance, PAT and NAPLAN alongside. Hover any name for an instant summary card; Ctrl+K finds anyone. Exportable as a polished PDF for meetings with families or support staff.

Insight for leadership

An Overview page that reads the story in plain English, Statistics dashboards, a Data wall heatmap of every subject by year level, multi-year Trends that follow cohorts (with comparable small multiples and timeline annotations), school targets drawn on the charts, and a growth-vs-achievement quadrant of every student.

Professional reports in seconds

One Reports page with every report and its options laid out: a whole-school overview pack (with a written digest), batch student PDFs (one per student or one combined file), Students to Watch and Subject Gap reports — plus branded, colour-coded PDF and Excel exports of any view, stamped with your school name.

Private, secure — and forgiving

Runs on one computer at your school — no cloud, no third-party hosting, no internet required. Per-teacher sign-ins with roles (imports and deletes stay admin-only), data encrypted at rest, password-protected AES-256 portable backups. Automatic snapshots before anything destructive, one-click Undo on imports and deletes, and a merge tool that re-joins a student's history if their code ever changes.

FAQ

Common questions

Where does our student data go?

Nowhere. It runs on a computer at your school and teachers use it through their browsers on the school network — no cloud, no third-party hosting, no internet required. Data is encrypted on the machine it lives on.

What data does it need?

The standard Compass semester report export (CSV), or a simple fill-in CSV template for schools on other reporting systems. One click to import — single semesters or a whole folder at once. Attendance reads the standard CASES21 half-day export; PAT reads ACER's exports (the per-test Group Report or the Data Explorer file); NAPLAN reads your school's CASES21/APS results CSV or an SSSR-style file. Attendance, PAT and NAPLAN imports show a match preview — who matched, who didn't — before anything lands.

Does it work for secondary schools?

Yes — Prep to Year 10, all Victorian Curriculum learning areas, primary and secondary.

What happens when the licence expires?

Your data stays yours. The app becomes read-only for imports, edits and exports until renewed — everything you've imported remains viewable.

What if we get a new computer?

Teachers’ computers don’t matter — everything lives on the one school machine it’s installed on. Moving that machine is a reinstall plus a password-protected backup restore.

How is it installed?

One professional Windows installer on a single school computer — a server or any always-on PC (Windows 10/11 or Windows Server, 64-bit). It starts automatically with the machine; teachers just need a browser and the address, nothing installed on their computers.

Can we try it first?

Yes — the app installs with a built-in sample dataset, so you can explore every feature before licensing. Activating your licence clears the sample data automatically. Email admin@vcdatatracker.com.au for the installer.

Is this a DET product?

No — it's independent software built for Victorian government school workflows.
