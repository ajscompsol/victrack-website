What it does

Purpose-built for Victorian Curriculum data

Compass-ready in one click

Imports the standard Compass semester report export (CSV) directly — no re-typing, no spreadsheet surgery. Prep to Year 10, every Victorian Curriculum learning area, primary and secondary. Re-importing a semester safely replaces it.

Growth you can see

Every result is banded against the semester-aware "at level" expectation across seven colour-coded bands. Compare any two points in time — the six-months-per-semester expectation scales automatically. "Made growth" percentages at a glance.

Every student's story

Click any name for their full history: every subject and semester, colour-coded, with summary statistics — exportable as a polished one-page PDF for meetings with families or support staff.

Trends & insight for leadership

Statistics dashboards by year level, subject or home group — plus multi-year Trends: follow cohorts over up to seven years, four measures, automatic insights flagging the strongest improvements and largest declines.

Professional reports in seconds

Branded, colour-coded PDF and Excel exports of any view — filtered exactly as on screen, colour key included, stamped with cohort year, semesters compared and filters applied. Copy any table straight into Word, Excel or email.

Private and secure by design

Runs entirely on school computers: no cloud, no accounts, no internet required. Encrypted at rest, with password-protected AES-256 portable backups and atomic saves — student data never leaves the school.

Trends — the flagship view

Seven years of history, one chart

  • Two lenses: Year levels over time and Follow cohorts (apparent-cohort method)
  • Four measures: % at/above, % below, average vs expected in months, % made growth
  • Compare year levels or subjects side by side
  • Interactive chart with instant hover details, plus a sortable table underneath

Whole-school views included: year-level tabs, Below/Above level watchlists, an "All semesters" side-by-side view, Excel-style searchable filters, multi-level sort and drag-select copy.

Privacy & security

Your student data stays at school. Full stop.

VC Tracker is a Windows desktop app — not a website, not a cloud service. There are no accounts, no sync and no telemetry. Nothing is sent anywhere, and the app doesn't need the internet at all.

  • 100% offline — no cloud, no accounts, no internet required
  • Encrypted at rest, tied to the Windows sign-in
  • Password-protected AES-256 portable backups
  • Atomic saves with rolling backup — your history is safe
  • Built for school IT — standard installer plus MSI for managed deployment, per-user install, offline activation; yearly versions install over the top and keep your data

FAQ

Common questions

Where does our student data go?

Nowhere. It's a desktop app — no cloud, no accounts, no internet required. Data is encrypted on the computer it lives on.

What data does it need?

The standard Compass semester report export (CSV). One click to import.

Does it work for secondary schools?

Yes — Prep to Year 10, all Victorian Curriculum learning areas, primary and secondary.

What happens when the licence expires?

Your data stays yours. The app becomes read-only for imports, edits and exports until renewed — everything you've imported remains viewable.

What if we get a new computer?

The site licence covers every school computer, and password-protected backups move your data across.

How is it installed?

A standard Windows installer, or an MSI for school-wide managed deployment by your technician. Windows 10/11 (64-bit); macOS on request.

Can we try it first?

Yes — a demonstration or a trial build with sample data is available on request.

Is this a DET product?

No — it's independent software built for Victorian government school workflows.
